# macOS Installation Guide

## Step 1: Downloading Ship of Harkinian

:::caution Important
This is the code that will interact with the Assets required in Step 2. This does NOT provide you with any Nintendo Licensed Assets.
:::

[Click here](https://github.com/HarbourMasters/Shipwright/releases/latest) to go to the releases page on GitHub, where you will find the links for **all latest versions** of Ship of Harkinian. Find and click the link that has **Mac** in the name.

**Extract the Zip file** into a Folder on your **Local Hard Drive** for later.

:::warning
Do NOT extract this to a temporary or a cloud drive location, things will break.
:::

Open the file named **Soh.dmg**, it will open a new window. Drag the file named **soh** into the **Applications** folder.

## Step 2: Obtaining a ROM (Legally)

Follow the steps outlined in the [ROM Dumping Guide](https://www.shipofharkinian.com/setup-guide/dump-guide) to **legally** obtain a **usable ROM** for Ship of Harkinian.

The ReadMe file **included with the download** mentioned in Step 1 lists out the **CRC/SHA1 Hash** and a website to verify your version. **Incorrect versions are not supported** and you will see an error or the game will not load.

## Step 3: Launching Ship of Harkinian

Assuming you correctly followed the instructions in Step 1 and Step 2, you are now ready for the last process.

Navigate to your **Applications** list. **Control-click** the **soh.app** file, then click **Open**.

Then click **Open** on the next prompt that appears.

### On macOS Ventura (13) and later:

You must first attempt to open the app, and dismiss the warning by clicking **Done**.

Then go to **System Settings > Privacy & Security** to approve the app.

Click **Open Anyway**.

:::info
This button is available for about an hour after you try to open the app.
:::

## Step 4: Extracting the Assets

Once the **soh.app** has been opened for the **first time**, you will be **greeted with a popup window**. Select **Yes** on this prompt.

At the next prompt, select **Yes** again.

Locate your **ROM that you dumped** from Step 2 and double click it to begin the **Asset Extraction** process.

This **Asset Extraction** process will happen in the background, and you will see a pop-up notification in the **top right corner**.

Once the process is complete, the pop-up will display a completion message and the game will launch.

**Congratulations, you can now play Ocarina of Time on Ship of Harkinian!**
