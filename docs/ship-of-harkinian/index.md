# Home

Ship of Harkinian is a PC port of The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, featuring enhanced graphics, quality of life improvements, and mod support.

- **Official Releases**: [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/HarbourMasters/Shipwright/releases/)
- **Discord Support**: [HM64 Discord](https://discord.com/channels/808039310850130000/1131920363115257896)
- **Find Mods**: [GameBanana](https://gamebanana.com/games/16121)

Ship of Harkinian is fully open source. Anyone can contribute to the project or create their own custom builds. The official repository can be found at [HarbourMasters/Shipwright](https://github.com/HarbourMasters/Shipwright).
